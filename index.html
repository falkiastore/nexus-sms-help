Core Strength

Google Sheets Backend

Zero server cost. Your data stays in your own Google Sheets. All logic runs via Google Apps Script — no hosting fees, no data leaks to third parties.

30-Second Attendance

Mark attendance for an entire class with one click. Supports Present, Absent, Late, and Leave statuses with configurable late cutoff times.

Predictive Fee Forecast

AI-powered monthly fee collection forecast. See how much you've collected, how much is projected, and whether you'll hit your target — before month-end.

PDF Report Cards & SLCs

Generate professional report cards for individual students or entire classes in bulk. School Leaving Certificates (SLC) with all student details ready to print.

Urdu-First Bilingual

Complete Urdu support with proper RTL layout. Student names, fee receipts, report cards — everything works in your language with proper Nastaliq font rendering.

Automated Backups

Schedule daily, weekly, or monthly CSV/ZIP backups delivered straight to your email. No manual effort — your data is always protected.

Smart Notifications

Automatic email notifications for fee payments, overdue fees, grade publishing, and absent students. Plus a live news ticker for school-wide announcements.

Enterprise-Grade Security

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nexus SMS.

Nexus SMS (School Management System) V2.0 is a complete, cloud-based school management platform built on Google Apps Script and Google Sheets. Unlike expensive SaaS products, it runs entirely on your own Google account — zero server costs, full data ownership, and no monthly fees per user.

Nexus SMS is currently in Free Beta. All 9 modules and features are available at no cost. After launch, affordable one-time or subscription plans will be offered. Contact us for early-adopter pricing.

Yes. Nexus SMS has full Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). Admins see and can do everything. Teachers can only access their assigned classes and see the attendance and grades modules. Accountants only see fee-related data and the finance dashboard. Each role has its own tailored dashboard.

Yes. Nexus SMS is fully bilingual with Urdu and English support. Student names, fee receipts, report cards, and system notifications can all be in Urdu. The interface supports proper RTL layout with Noto Nastaliq Urdu font rendering.

Yes. Passwords are hashed with SHA-256 + salt. Sessions are secured with httpOnly cookies. There is built-in account lockout protection and force-password-change on first login. Each school's data is fully isolated in your own Google Sheets — no other school can access your data.

Yes. Nexus SMS generates professional PDF report cards for individual students or entire classes in bulk. School Leaving Certificates (SLC) can also be generated with full student information. Custom grading scales (A+, A, B, C, D, F) with configurable min/max marks are fully supported.

You can schedule automatic backups (daily, weekly, or monthly) from the Settings module. All school data is exported as CSV or ZIP and delivered to your email. If the file is too large, a Google Drive link is sent instead. No manual steps required after initial setup.

Yes. Nexus SMS is fully responsive and works on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile phone. It is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA), so you can install it on your phone's home screen and use it like a native app, even with limited internet connectivity.
