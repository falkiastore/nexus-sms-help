Module Overview

The Students module is the heart of Nexus SMS. It allows you to register new students, maintain detailed profiles, manage parent/guardian information, and track academic enrollment status.

Key Procedures

1

Adding a New Student

Navigate to the Students module and click on "Add New Student". Fill in the mandatory fields: Name, Date of Birth, Class, and Parent Contact. The system will automatically generate a unique Student ID.

[Screenshot: New Student Form]
2

Searching and Filtering

Use the search bar at the top of the student registry to quickly find a student by name or ID. You can also filter the list by Class or Section to narrow down your selection.

[Screenshot: Filter Controls]
3

Updating Student Records

Click the "Edit" icon next to any student record to modify their information. Ensure you save changes to keep the Registry up to date.

Pro Tip

Always ensure the Parent Mobile Number is entered correctly to enable automated SMS notifications for attendance and fees.
