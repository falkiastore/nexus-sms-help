Student Management Manual

Everything you need to know about registering and managing your student body.

Procedural Step 1

Accessing the Registry

Navigate to the 'Students' module from the primary sidebar. This main view displays your entire student registry. You can use the smart-filter bar at the top to instantly narrow down students by Admission Year, Current Class, or Academic Status (Active/Withdrawn).

Procedural Step 2

The New Admission Workflow

To register a new student, click the high-priority 'Add New' button. The system will guide you through a three-stage wizard:

• Personal Details: Name, DOB, medical info.
• Enrollment: Class assignment and ID generation.
• Parental Linkage: Connecting the student to a new or existing guardian account.

[Wizard UI: Student Admission Flow]
Procedural Step 3

Managing Parent Communications

Each student profile is tethered to a Parent/Guardian contact. It is critical to ensure the 'Primary Contact Number' is formatted correctly to enable the Nexus Notification Engine for automated SMS attendance and fee alerts.

[Form Detail: Contact Phone Field Explainer]
Procedural Step 4

Bulk Data Management

Need to move an entire class to the next academic level? Use the 'Bulk Operations' menu to perform class promotions, section shifts, or to generate bulk ID cards for a whole grade level at once.

[Action Pad: Bulk Operations Menu]

DID YOU KNOW? SMART SEARCH

You can search for a student using just their Parent's Phone Number or the student's unique Roll Number directly in the global search bar at any time.
