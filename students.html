Screens and Workflows

Each card tells you exactly what the screenshot should show. Add images later with the same file names in the images folder.

The Giant Book of Friends This is our master list where every student's name, class, and happy news are kept safe in our big school book! It's like a big school book that remembers everyone, helping teachers and staff stay connected with every single friend in the school family. With this registry, no one is ever forgotten, and every important detail is always ready when we need to celebrate a success or plan a new adventure.
Magic Magnifying Glass Use our special magic filters to find exactly the friend you're looking for without searching everywhere! It works just like a game of hide and seek, but the magnifying glass always wins by showing you the right class or name in a flash. This tool makes it super easy for the school team to organize lists and find information quickly, so they can spend more time focusing on fun and learning.
Welcome to the Family! A friendly admission form designed to help new friends join our school and start their amazing adventure with us! It asks for all the important bits like names and birthdays to make sure every new member fits right into the family. By filling this out, you're opening the door to a world of discovery, high-fives, and wonderful memories that will last a lifetime in our magical school.
Which Grade Are You? Pick the perfect grade for our new friends so they can sit with their classmates and have lots of fun learning together! This dropdown menu makes it simple to place every student in the right room with the right teacher, ensuring they are always in the best spot to grow. It's a key part of our school's organization, helping everyone find their place in the big, colorful puzzle of our learning community.
Boy or Girl? Keep our school records perfect so every friend feels at home and we can plan the best games and activities for everyone! This little selection helps us understand our school family better, making sure we have everything needed for both boys and girls to shine. Accurate details help the school feel like a true home where every individual student is recognized and cared for with a special touch.
Secret Smile Menu A tiny action menu for every student that lets you quickly see their profile or change their details whenever something exciting happens! Whether a friend moves to a new house or gets a new phone number, this menu makes it easy to keep the 'Giant Book of Friends' updated and accurate. It's the secret key to keeping our school information fresh and helpful, all with a simple click and a smile.
