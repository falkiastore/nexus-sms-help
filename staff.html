Staff Management

Register, organize, and manage your teaching and administrative workforce. Control access, assign classes, and maintain complete staff profiles.

Phase 1: Registry

Staff Registry Table

The Staff module opens to a data grid listing all registered employees. Each row shows the staff member's name, email, role, designation, phone number, and status.

  • Search: Filter staff by name using the search bar
  • Status Indicator: Green for Active, Red for Inactive accounts
  • Export: Download the full staff directory as a spreadsheet
[Image: staff_registry_table.png]
Phase 2: Registration

Adding New Staff

Click + Add Staff to open the registration form. Fill in the staff member's details:

  • Full Name: Official name as it should appear in the system
  • Email: Login credential — must be unique across all staff
  • Role: Admin, Teacher, or Accountant (determines module access)
  • Designation: Job title (e.g., "Senior Maths Teacher")
  • Phone: Contact number for records
Default Password New staff accounts are created with a temporary password of 123456. They will be required to change it on first login.
[Image: staff_add_form.png]
Phase 3: RBAC

Role Assignment & Permissions

The role assigned to a staff member determines what they can see and do in Nexus SMS:

  • Admin: Full access — all modules including Settings, Staff Management, and System Configuration
  • Teacher: Dashboard (teacher view), Attendance marking, Grades entry
  • Accountant: Dashboard (finance view), Fees module, Export
Security Note Only Admin-role users can access the Staff module, Settings, and Student management. Choose roles carefully.

Phase 4: Management

Staff Actions

Each staff record has an Actions Menu with the following options:

  • ✏️ Edit: Update name, phone, designation, or role
  • 🔑 Reset Password: Force password reset — sets the temporary password and flags the account for mandatory change on next login
  • 🔴 Inactivate: Deactivate the account — prevents login while preserving historical data
[Image: staff_actions_menu.png]
Phase 5: Teaching

Class Assignment

Teachers can be assigned to specific class groups. When a teacher logs into Nexus, they only see their assigned classes in the Attendance and Grades modules, streamlining their workflow.

Multi-Class Support A teacher can be assigned to multiple classes simultaneously. They'll see all their classes in the dropdowns when marking attendance or entering grades.
