Staff & HR Operational Manual

Manage your school's most valuable asset—your professional staff and faculty.

Step 1

The Staff Registry

Access the Staff module to view a comprehensive list of all teaching and non-teaching personnel. You can find staff members by Department (e.g., Mathematics, Administration, Security) or by their unique Staff ID. The registry provides a high-level view of their contact details and current employment status.

🧑‍🏫
Step 2

Onboarding New Personnel

When hiring, use the 'Add Staff' wizard. It is essential to capture:

• Professional Info: Qualification, Designation, and Department.
• Personal Details: Secure storage of NIC/ID card numbers and address.
• Contact: Mobile numbers for receiving internal system notifications.

+ Upload Staff Photograph
Step 3

Departmental Hierarchy

Organize your faculty by creating custom 'Departments' in the System Settings. You can then assign Department Heads to oversee academic performance and attendance for their respective teams.

[Diagram: School Organizational Structure]

HR TIP: EMERGENCY CONTACTS

We recommend always populating the 'Emergency Contact' field for staff members. In the event of a medical emergency on campus, this information is instantly accessible to administrators from the staff registry.
