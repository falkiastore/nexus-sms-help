Attendance Mastery Guide

Efficiently track daily presence and keep parents informed with automated alerts.

Operational Step 1

Sheet Selection & Loading

Enter the attendance module and select the specific Class and Section. Choose the target date (today's date is selected by default). The system will instantly fetch the student roster based on the current academic enrollment.

[UI Preview: Filter Selection Bar]
Operational Step 2

Smart Marking & Bulk Actions

By default, all students are marked as 'Present' to save time. Use the toggle buttons next to a student's name to change their status to 'Absent', 'Late', or 'Leave'. You can also use the 'Bulk Absent' tool for specific groups during events.

[Action Pad: Attendance Marking Interface]
Operational Step 3

Automated SMS Dispatch

Once you click 'Save Attendance', the Nexus Notification Engine kicks in. If enabled, parent contacts for all students marked 'Absent' will receive an immediate SMS alert: "Dear Parent, [StudentName] is absent today from school."

[Infographic: SMS Alert Logic Flow]
Operational Step 4

Consolidated Monthly Reports

Navigating to the 'Reports' section from within the attendance module allows you to generate PDF registers for any month. These reports highlight total days present, percentage of attendance, and frequent late-comers.

[Report Sample: Monthly Attendance PDF]

CRITICAL: DOUBLE-SAVE GUARD

Nexus SMS features a safety lock to prevent duplicate attendance marking for the same class on the same day. If you need to correct an entry, use the 'Modify' feature instead of adding a new record.
