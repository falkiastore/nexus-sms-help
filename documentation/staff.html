Staff Management

The complete guide to HR operations, organizational hierarchy, and granular access control for your faculty and administration.

Phase 1: HR Registry

The Professional Roster

Maintain a secure, centralized database of all institutional personnel. The Staff Registry allows for instant filtering by Department, Designation, or Appointment Date.

HR Tip: Document Management You can attach digital copies of staff qualifications and employment contracts directly to their professional profile for easy administrative retrieval.
[Placeholder: staff_registry_view.png]
Phase 2: Security

Role-Based Access (RBAC)

Security is paramount. Nexus SMS employs a sophisticated RBAC system to ensure staff members only access tools relevant to their position.

  • Teacher Role: Restricted to attendance, marks entry, and student reports.
  • Accountant Role: Focused on fee collection, ledger audit, and financial reporting.
  • Admin Role: Full architectural control over the entire system.
[Placeholder: rbac_permission_grid.png]
Phase 3: Hierarchy

Departmental Structure

Define your school's organizational chart by creating custom departments. Assigning staff to departments enables streamlined internal communication and departmental reporting.

Administrative Note Deactivating a staff account will instantly revoke all system access across all devices but will preserve their historical records for audit purposes.
