Financial Control

Audit with confidence. Total control over fee structures, automated monthly billing, and real-time revenue intelligence.

Phase 1: Setup

Fee Head Architecture

Financial accuracy begins with correctly defined Fee Heads. You can configure recurring charges (Tuition) and one-time chargers (Admission/Activity) across different student categories.

Financial Tip: Bulk Assignment Nexus allows you to define a 'Master Fee Plan' for an entire class level, which then propagates to all enrolled students automatically.
Phase 2: Billing

The Batch Billing Engine

At the start of each month, the Nexus Billing Engine processes thousands of student accounts. It calculates arrears, applies current charges, and factors in scholarship deductions.

  • Challan Generation: Batch-generate PDF challans for parent distribution.
  • Arrears Logic: Unpaid dues are carried forward automatically to the next month's totals.

Phase 3: Audit

Collection Intelligence

Monitor your school's liquidity with real-time collection dashboards. Identify payment trends and generate Defaulter Lists with a single click.

Audit Safety Once a transaction is finalized and a receipt is issued, any corrections require administrative 'Voiding' rights to maintain a tamper-proof audit trail.
