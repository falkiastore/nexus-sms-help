My Account

Manage your personal profile, update your password, and maintain your professional presence within Nexus SMS.

Profile

Your Staff Profile

The My Account module displays your complete professional profile as recorded in the Staff sheet. This includes your name, email, role, designation, phone number, and joining date.

  • Full Name: Your official name as registered by the Admin
  • Login Email: The email used for authentication (cannot be changed here)
  • Role: Admin, Teacher, or Accountant — assigned by administrator
  • Status: Active or Inactive account status

Edit

Updating Your Information

You can update certain fields of your profile directly from the My Account page. Editable fields include your phone number, designation, and profile photo.

Photo Upload Upload a professional photo via URL. The photo is stored as a reference and appears in your profile card and sidebar avatar throughout the app.

Security

Changing Your Password

You can change your password at any time from the My Account page. You'll need to provide your current password, then enter and confirm a new password.

Important Rules The new password must be at least 6 characters. After changing, your session is refreshed automatically — no need to log in again.
