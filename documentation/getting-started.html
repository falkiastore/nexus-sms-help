Getting Started

Your complete onboarding guide. From first login to a fully operational school management system in under 30 minutes.

Step 1: Understand

What is Nexus SMS?

Nexus SMS is a cloud-powered School Management System built on Google Sheets and Google Apps Script. It provides a complete suite of tools for managing students, staff, attendance, fees, academics, and communications — all from a single web application.

  • Zero Installation: Runs entirely in your browser via Google Apps Script
  • Google Sheets Backend: Your data lives securely in Google Sheets you own
  • Role-Based Access: Admin, Teacher, and Accountant roles with tailored dashboards
  • Multi-Tenant: Licensed per school with centralized license management
[Image: dashboard_kpi_cards.png]
Step 2: Login

Your First Login

Navigate to your school's Nexus SMS web app URL. You'll be presented with the Login Screen. Enter the email address registered in the Staff sheet and the temporary password provided by your administrator.

Default Password The initial temporary password for all new accounts is 123456. You will be required to change it immediately upon first login.
[Image: login_screen.png]
Step 3: Secure

Force Password Change

On your first login, the system will redirect you to the Password Change Screen. You must set a new password (minimum 6 characters) before accessing any modules.

Security Requirement You cannot skip the password change step. This ensures all accounts have unique, secure passwords from day one.
[Image: force_password_change.png]
Step 4: Configure

Administrator Setup Checklist

Once logged in as Admin, follow this sequence to get your school fully operational:

  • 1. Settings → School Profile: Set your school name, address, and upload your logo
  • 2. Settings → Classes: Create your class groups (e.g., Grade-01 A, Grade-02)
  • 3. Staff Module: Add your teachers and accountants with their login emails
  • 4. Students Module: Register your students and assign them to classes
  • 5. Settings → Fee Structure: Define fee heads and monthly charges
  • 6. Settings → SMS: Configure SMS provider for attendance notifications
[Image: settings_school_profile.png]
Step 5: Navigate

Understanding the Sidebar

The app uses a sidebar navigation to switch between modules. Your available modules depend on your role:

  • Admin: Dashboard, Students, Staff, Fees, Grades, Attendance, Notifications, Export, Settings
  • Teacher: Dashboard, Attendance, Grades
  • Accountant: Dashboard, Fees, Export
Quick Tip The Dashboard is your home screen. It automatically loads after login and provides a high-level overview of your school's operational status.
