Reporting Hub

Turn institutional data into professional intelligence. Generate branded PDF documents and conduct deep-dive financial audits with ease.

Phase 1: Publication

High-Fidelity PDF Generation

Nexus SMS features a high-performance PDF engine. You can generate branded Student ID Cards, Certificates, and Official Transcripts that are instantly ready for high-resolution printing.

Reporting Tip: Branding Sync Ensure your school's official header and logo are updated in the Global Settings to maintain a consistent professional look across all generated documents.
[Placeholder: pdf_document_sample.png]
Phase 2: Analytics

Excel & CSV Data Bridge

For custom analytics or administrative audits, Nexus allows you to export any data grid directly into Microsoft Excel. This includes filtered student rosters, attendance logs, and staff records.

  • Smart Export: The export engine respects your active filters, allowing you to download specific subsets of data.
  • Interoperability: Formatted specifically for seamless import into external accounting or BI software.
[Placeholder: data_export_ui.png]
Phase 3: Audit

Revenue & Collection Summaries

Gain total transparency over your institution's finances. Generate daily collection summaries that categorize revenue by payment method (Cash, Bank, Online) and highlight fee reversals for internal audit logs.

Audit Requirement We recommend performing a Weekly Revenue Reconciliation report to cross-reference system transactions with your physical bank statements.
