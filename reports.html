Overview

Nexus SMS provides powerful tools to transform your raw data into actionable reports. You can generate PDF documents for immediate printing or export data to Excel for further analysis.

📄

PDF Generation

Create high-quality PDF reports for attendance, fee receipts, and report cards.

📊

Excel Export

Download entire datasets (Students, Staff, Fees) into CSV/Excel formats.

📈

Financial Reports

Generate monthly collection summaries and arrear lists for auditing.

How to Export Data

1

Select Report Type

Navigate to the Export module and choose the category of data you wish to retrieve (e.g., Student List or Fee Arrears).

2

Apply Filters

Refine your data by selecting Date Ranges, Classes, or specific Payment Statuses to ensure your report is accurate.

[Screenshot: Report Filter Options]
3

Download

Click "Generate" to preview the data, and then select your preferred format (PDF or Excel) to save the file to your device.
