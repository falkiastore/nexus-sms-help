Login & Authentication

Understand how Nexus SMS secures your institution's data with password-based authentication, session management, and mandatory password policies.

Authentication

The Login Screen

Nexus SMS uses a custom password-based authentication system. When you open the app URL, you'll see the Login screen where you enter your registered email and password.

  • Login ID: Your email address as registered in the Staff sheet
  • Password: Your personal password (temporary on first login)
  • Multi-Tenant: The system automatically finds your school's data based on your email
[Image: login_screen.png]
Security

Mandatory Password Change

First-time users or users whose passwords have been reset by an Admin are required to set a new password before accessing the system. This enforces zero shared-password risk.

Password Rules New passwords must be at least 6 characters. The system hashes all passwords with SHA-256 and a unique salt per user — plaintext passwords are never stored.
[Image: force_password_change.png]
Sessions

Session Management

After successful login, Nexus creates a secure session valid for 8 hours. The session is stored across multiple layers (cache + persistent properties) for reliability.

  • Duration: 8-hour session lifetime from login
  • Auto-Redirect: Expired sessions redirect you back to the login screen
  • Multi-Tab Safe: Sessions work across multiple browser tabs
Mobile Access You can bookmark the app URL with your session ID for quick access on mobile devices. Sessions persist even if you close and reopen the browser.

Roles

Role-Based Dashboard Loading

After login, Nexus automatically loads the dashboard appropriate for your role. Each role sees a different set of KPIs and modules:

  • Admin Dashboard: Full metrics — students, staff, revenue, attendance, grade insights, forecasting
  • Teacher Dashboard: Class count, student count, attendance status, pending grades
  • Accountant Dashboard: Total collected, total due, today's collection, overdue students
