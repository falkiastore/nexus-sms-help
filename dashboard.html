Screens and Workflows

Each card tells you exactly what the screenshot should show. Add images later with the same file names in the images folder.

Super School Stats Get a quick overview of your school's vital signs. This dashboard shows you exactly how many students are learning today, tracks your teacher team's attendance, and provides real-time updates on school activities. It's like having a high-tech command center where every important detail is just a glance away, helping you stay on top of everything happening in your magical world of learning.
Shiny Stars & Helping Hands Find out who is doing great and who needs a little extra help to shine in their studies! This section allows you to celebrate every success together as a school community, ensuring no student is left behind. By identifying those who might need more support, you can provide the magic touch needed to help every child reach their full potential and become a superstar learner.
The Piggy Bank Circle A colorful circle that shows if the school's piggy bank is filling up with collected fees! It helps the school team understand how much treasure is available to buy more toys, books, and exciting equipment for the students. This simple visual is a great way to keep everyone updated on the school's financial health, making it fun and easy to manage the resources needed for a wonderful learning journey.
Daily Presence Parade A fun bar chart showing exactly who came to school to join the learning fun today! We love seeing all our friends every morning, and this chart helps us keep track of the daily attendance parade across all classes. It's a key part of making sure everyone feels welcome and included, helping the school family stay close-knit and ensuring that every student is ready for a day of discovery.
Magic Fee Forecast Our smart app uses a bit of magic to guess how much more the piggy bank will grow by the end of the month! By looking at past trends, it helps the school plan for future adventures and ensures there's always enough treasure for new projects. It's like having a crystal ball for the school's finances, giving you the confidence to make big plans for a brighter and even more magical future.
More Friends Every Day! Watch how our school family is getting bigger and bigger as more and more friends join us to learn and play! This growth trend shows how popular the school is becoming, with new students arriving to share in the magic of discovery. It's a wonderful way to celebrate the school's success and prepare for an even bigger, more exciting future where every new friend adds a unique sparkle to our community.
Important School Whispers Stay updated with all the new news through these important school whispers! These little notes tell you everything you need to know about upcoming school events, special announcements, and magical surprises. By keeping everyone in the loop, we make sure that no one misses out on the fun, helping to build a well-informed and excited school community where every piece of news is a reason to celebrate.
Magic Teacher View A special secret screen designed just for teachers to help them mark your daily high-fives and test scores super fast! It's a powerful tool that gives educators more time to focus on teaching and playing, making the school day even more magical for everyone. This specialized view simplifies their work, ensuring that all student successes are recorded accurately and celebrated with the care they deserve.
Treasurer's Gold Map This is Where the school's treasure manager keeps everything in perfect balance! It provides a bird's-eye view of all the school's gold coins and expenditures, ensuring that there's always enough for every magical lesson and exciting school project. By managing the school's wealth with such care, the treasurer helps make sure that the learning adventure continues without a single worry.
