Dashboard Mastery

Your institutional command center. Monitor vital statistics, track financial health, and observe academic trends in real-time.

Phase 1: Metrics

Real-Time Vital Counters

The dashboard primary tier features dynamic counter cards. These provide a live census of your institution—tracking Active Enrollments, Daily Attendance, and Financial Collection status at a glance.

Efficiency Pro-Tip Clicking any metric card will instantly trigger a "Deep Dive" report, taking you directly to the underlying data records for that specific metric.
[Placeholder: dashboard_counters_preview.png]
Phase 2: Visuals

Operational Trend Analytics

Observe the pulse of your school through high-fidelity charts. The central analytics panel displays attendance cycles and fee collection velocity, allowing for informed administrative decision-making.

  • Comparative Views: Compare performance across different classes or academic sessions.
  • Export Ready: Every chart can be instantly converted to a high-resolution image for board presentations.
[Placeholder: analytic_trends_chart.png]
Phase 3: Awareness

The Live Activity Stream

On the dashboard periphery, the System Ledger logs every major action—from new student registrations to successful SMS transmissions—providing a timeline of institutional activity.

Audit Note The activity stream is role-filtered. Users will only see logs pertaining to their specific administrative permissions.
