Dashboard Operational Guide

Master the command center of your school management system.

Step 1

The Real-Time Snapshot

Upon logging in, your primary view is the Dashboard. This screen provides an immediate, live look at your school's vital statistics. No manual refreshing is required; the data updates dynamically as students register or payments are processed.

Step 2

Monitoring Key Metrics

Observe the four primary counter cards at the top. These track Total Students (Active), Staff Presence (Today), Monthly Collection (Revenue), and Academic Performance trends. Clicking any card will take you directly to the detailed report for that metric.

[Infographic: Metric Card Explainer]
Step 3

Academic Performance Chart

The central line chart displays grading trends across the school. You can filter this view by Class or Subject to identify academic strengths and weaknesses in your student body at a glance.

[Chart: Academic Trends View]
Step 4

Recent Notifications Feed

On the right sidebar, the 'Activity Feed' highlights the latest system events—such as high-value fee collections, automated SMS failures, or emergency staff leave requests.

[Sidebar: Activity Log Preview]

PRO TIP: DASHBOARD DRILL-DOWN

Did you know? Double-clicking any data point on the Dashboard charts will open the specific student or transaction record in a modal for instant editing without leaving the page.
