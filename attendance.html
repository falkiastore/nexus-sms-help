Screens and Workflows

Each card tells you exactly what the screenshot should show. Add images later with the same file names in the images folder.

The Presence Parade This is a colorful and informative bar chart showing exactly who came to school to join the fun today! We love seeing all our friends every morning, and this visual guide helps the school team identify attendance patterns across different classes in a snap. It's a key part of maintaining a vibrant learning environment where every student's presence is celebrated as a step towards a brighter future.
Teacher's High-Five Screen A specialized screen designed for teachers to mark students as 'Present' with a quick, easy tap—just like giving a digital high-five! This intuitive interface simplifies the morning routine, allowing educators to focus more on their students and less on paperwork. By making attendance tracking so simple, we ensure that every child's arrival is recorded with a smile, fostering a positive and organized start to every school day.
The Holiday Countdown A special school calendar where we mark all the fun holidays and important breaks so everyone knows when it's time to rest and play! This feature ensures that the school schedule is always clear and transparent for both teachers and families, helping everyone plan for upcoming adventures. It's an essential tool for keeping the school's heartbeat synchronized, making sure that every holiday is anticipated with excitement and joy.
Classroom Map A detailed list that shows exactly which students belong in which classroom, making the morning roll-call perfectly organized and stress-free! This digital map helps teachers manage their class lists efficiently, ensuring that every student is in the right place for their magical lessons. By keeping our classrooms well-organized, we create a stable and supportive environment where students can focus on their studies and building wonderful friendships.
