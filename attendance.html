Overview

Nexus SMS provides a streamlined way to record and track daily attendance. The system supports bulk marking, attendance reports, and automated SMS alerts to parents of absent students.

📊

Bulk Marking

Quickly mark entire classes as Present or Absent in one go.

📱

SMS Alerts

Automatically notify parents via SMS as soon as a student is marked absent.

📈

Reporting

Generate monthly and yearly attendance summaries for any student or class.

Marking Attendance

1

Select Class and Date

Go to the Attendance module, select the Class, Section, and the current Date. The list of students will populate automatically.

[Screenshot: Attendance Filters]
2

Assign Status

By default, all students are marked "Present". Simply toggle the status to "Absent", "Late", or "Leave" for those who are not present.

3

Save and Notify

Review the list and click "Save Attendance". If configured, the system will immediately queue SMS alerts for absent students.
