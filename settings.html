System Configuration Manual

Customize Nexus SMS branding, academic years, and system parameters.

Configuration 1

Official Branding & Identity

Your school's identity is central to all documentation. In Settings > School Profile, upload your official logo (high-resolution PNG/JPG recommended) and enter your school's full name, address, and contact details. This info is automatically injected into all PDF receipts and report cards.

Configuration 2

Academic Calendar & Holidays

Define your school's operating days. Use the Holidays sub-module to mark public holidays, winter/summer vacations, and unplanned leaves. The system uses this calendar to automatically calculate student attendance percentages and adjust fee due dates.

License & Usage Caps

Monitor your Nexus SMS license. View your current active student limit and subscription expiry date. If you need to increase your school's capacity, you can initiate a 'Capacity Upgrade' request directly from the License Dashboard.
