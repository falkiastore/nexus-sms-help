Overview

The Settings module is where you configure the foundational data for your school. This includes school branding, academic sessions, holiday calendars, and department structures.

🎨

School Profile

Upload your school logo, set the name, address, and contact details for headers.

🗓️

Sessions & Classes

Define academic years and manage the list of classes and sections available.

🔑

License & Security

Manage your software license and configure user roles and permissions.

Core Configurations

1

Setting School Identity

Go to Settings > School Info. Upload a high-resolution logo and enter your official school name. This info will appear on all receipts and report cards.

2

Managing Academic Calendar

Use the Holidays sub-module to mark dates when the school is closed. This will automatically adjust attendance calculations and fee due dates.

[Screenshot: Holiday Calendar]
3

Theme Customization

Choose between light and dark modes, and select your primary brand color to customize the application's appearance.
