Financial & Fee Audit Guide

Master the school's financial backbone—from fee structures to revenue reporting.

Phase 1: Setup

Defining Fee Structures

Before collecting payments, you must define your 'Fee Heads' (e.g., Monthly Tuition, Admission Fee, Transport, Library). Assign these rates per Class or Level to ensure the system generates accurate challans for the entire student body.

Phase 2: Invoicing

The Monthly Billing Cycle

At the start of each month, use the 'Generate Collective Invoices' tool. The system will calculate arrears, apply current month fees, and add any specific fines or discounts for thousands of students in seconds.

[Infographic: Invoicing Logic & Arrears Calculation]
Phase 3: Collection

Recording Payments & Receipts

When a parent submits a fee, search for the student via ID or Mobile Number. The system displays all pending dues. Enter the amount received, select the payment bank/cash head, and click 'Post Transaction' to generate a thermal or A4 receipt instantly.

[Form Detail: Transaction Posting Screen]
Phase 4: Auditing

Revenue Insights & Arrears

Use the 'Financial Summary' dashboard to monitor your collection vs. projection. You can generate 'Defaulter Lists' grouped by Class and send automated 'Fee Due' SMS alerts to parents with a single click.

[Chart View: Arrears vs. Collection Trends]

PRO TIP: SCHOLARSHIP MANAGEMENT

You can apply recurring 'Concession Profiles' to scholarship students. Once set, the system will automatically deduct the discount amount every month without manual intervention.
