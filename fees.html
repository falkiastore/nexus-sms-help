Screens and Workflows

Each card tells you exactly what the screenshot should show. Add images later with the same file names in the images folder.

School Fee Recipes This is where we set up the correct fee amounts for each class, just like following a perfect recipe for a delicious school cake! Each class has its own special blend of ingredients, and this configuration ensures that everything is measured perfectly for the school's financial health. It's the foundation of our treasure management, helping to ensure that the resources are always available for new books, fun games, and a world of learning adventures.
The Big Fee Checklist A clear and organized list that helps us see at a glance which friends have already filled their piggy banks and who might need a gentle little reminder! This registry keeps everyone on track, making it easy for the school team to manage collections with care and friendliness. By keeping such a close eye on our 'Big Fee Checklist,' we ensure that the school's treasure is always growing, ready to support every magical discovery our students make.
Adding Coins to the Bank A quick and easy popup window to record whenever a friend brings their school fees, making the school's piggy bank grow larger instantly! This simple tool ensures that every contribution is captured accurately, celebrating the support of every family in our school community. It's a key part of our daily treasure-keeping, making the process of receiving and recording fees feel like a positive part of our shared learning journey.
The Golden Thank-You Note A beautiful and official receipt that we give to parents as a golden 'Thank You' note for helping our school family grow and thrive! This document is a formal record of their contribution, reflecting our appreciation for their support and investment in their child's magical future. It provides peace of mind for families, showing that their fees are being managed with the highest level of care and professional respect in our wonderful school.
Colorful Fee Donut A bright, happy, and color-coded chart that shows at a single glance how much the school has collected today compared to our goals—it's as easy as looking at a yummy donut! This visual summary helps the school's treasure managers understand our progress quickly, turning complex numbers into a simple and delightful picture. It's a wonderful way to celebrate our growing success, ensuring that we are always moving forward on our path to creating the best school experience possible.
