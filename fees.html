Financial Management

Complete control over fee structures, payment recording, collection tracking, and financial forecasting — all automated within a single module.

Foundation

Fee Structure Setup

Before collecting any fees, define your Fee Heads in Settings → Fee Structure. Fee heads represent the different charge categories (Tuition, Transport, Lab Fees, etc.).

  • Recurring Fees: Monthly charges that auto-apply to billing cycles
  • One-Time Fees: Admission charges, exam fees, etc.
  • Per-Class Rates: Different fee amounts for different class levels
[Image: fees_structure_setup.png]
Ledger

The Fee Register

The Fee Register is a comprehensive student-wise ledger showing total due, total collected, and outstanding balance for every student. Filter by class to focus on specific groups.

Smart Summary The register header shows aggregate totals: Total Fee, Total Collected, Total Due, and the number of students with outstanding balances.
[Image: fees_register_table.png]
Collection

Recording Payments

Click the payment icon next to any student to open the payment recording dialog. Enter the amount, select the payment method (Cash, Bank Transfer, Online), and submit.

  • Partial Payments: Accept any amount — the balance updates automatically
  • Transaction Log: Each payment creates a timestamped entry in the Fee Transactions sheet
  • Instant Update: Dashboard KPIs and charts reflect payments immediately
[Image: fees_record_payment.png]
Analytics

Collection Intelligence

The Dashboard's financial section provides two critical visualizations from your fee data:

  • Fees Donut: Collected vs Overdue with center collection percentage
  • Predictive Forecast: Month-end collection projection based on payment velocity
Forecasting

Revenue Audit Forecast

The Predictive Collection Forecast analyzes your current month's daily collection rate and extrapolates to estimate month-end totals. Three bars compare: Already Collected, Projected Total, and the Target.

Forecast Accuracy Forecasts are most accurate after the first week of each month when sufficient data points establish a reliable velocity pattern.
Documentation

Challans & Receipts

Generate print-ready fee receipts branded with your school's logo, name, and contact information as configured in Settings. Each receipt includes the transaction details, student information, and a unique reference number.

Branding Sync Receipts automatically use the school logo, name, and primary color from your Settings → School Profile configuration.
