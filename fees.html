Overview

The Financials module handles all fee-related activities, from defining fee structures to processing student payments and generating receipts.

💳

Fee Structures

Define Monthly, Admission, Exam, and Transport fees per class.

📄

Invoicing

Automatically generate monthly fee challans for all active students.

🏦

Payment Tracking

Record payments, track arrears, and generate financial reports.

Processing Payments

1

Find Student

Enter the Student ID or scan the QR code on the fee challan to pull up the student's pending dues.

2

Collect Fee

Select the months being paid for, enter the amount received, and specify the payment method (Cash/Bank).

[Screenshot: Collection Form]
3

Generate Receipt

Save the transaction to generate a printable receipt. The system will update the student's ledger instantly.
