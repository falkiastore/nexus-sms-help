Notification Engine Guide

Learn how to automate school-to-parent communication using SMS and system alerts.

Capability 1

Automated Absence Alerts

The most widely used feature of Nexus SMS is the automated absence alert. When attendance is saved, the system identifies all students marked 'Absent' and queues an SMS to their primary guardian. This ensures 100% transparency between school and home.

Dear Parent, your child Salman is marked ABSENT today (Mar 14). Please contact the office for any queries. - Nexus SMS

Capability 2

Fee Collection Confirmations

Building trust with parents starts with timely financial updates. Whenever a fee is posted, an automated confirmation SMS is sent, including the Amount Received and current Arrears Balance.

Capability 3

Bulk Broadcasting

Need to announce a sudden holiday or an upcoming parent-teacher meeting? Use the 'SMS Broadcast' module. You can select entire classes, staff members, or the whole school to send a custom announcement in seconds.

Always check the 'SMS Logs' in the Notifications module to verify delivery status. If a message fails, it's usually due to an incorrect phone format (+ CountryCode is required).
