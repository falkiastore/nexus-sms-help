Overview

Nexus SMS keeps parents and staff informed through a robust notification system. This includes automated SMS for attendance and fees, as well as manual broadcasts for school news.

🚫

Absence Alerts

Real-time SMS sent to parents when a student is marked absent.

💸

Fee Reminders

Automatic notifications for pending dues and payment confirmations.

📢

Broadcasting

Send custom announcements to groups of parents or staff members.

Messaging Workflow

1

Select Recipients

Choose whether to send a message to a Specific Class, All Students, or individual staff members.

2

Compose Message

Enter your announcement. The system supports dynamic tags like [StudentName] to personalize the message.

[Screenshot: Compose Notification]
3

Send and Track

Review the SMS count and click "Broadcast". You can track the delivery status in the Notification Logs.
