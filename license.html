License Management

Secure your institutional investment. Master the activation workflow, renewal cycles, and multi-school license distribution.

Phase 1: Activation

Core Product Security

Every instance of Nexus SMS is secured by a unique Institutional Key. This key binds your data to your specific high-performance database instance, ensuring absolute privacy and data integrity.

Security Note Never share your activation key outside of the Super Admin tier. Lost keys can only be recovered through official support verification.

Phase 2: Continuity

Subscription & Renewal

Continuity is key to smooth school operations. Nexus provides automated 30-day expiry warnings on the main dashboard to ensure you have ample time to process renewals.

  • Grace Period: We provide a 7-day administrative grace period post-expiry for critical data retrieval.
  • Real-time Update: License renewals are pushed to your institution instantly upon payment verification.

Multi-School Control
