License Management

Secure and manage your software subscription

Overview

Nexus SMS uses a secure licensing system to ensure your school's data remains protected and your subscription is always up to date. The License Control module allows you to view your current plan, expiry date, and upgrade your limits.

🛡️

Activation

Activate your software using the unique License Key provided during onboarding.

📅

Expiry Tracking

Monitor your license validity and receive alerts before your subscription ends.

🚀

Usage Limits

View your student and staff limits based on your current subscription plan.

Management Steps

1

Check License Status

Navigate to Settings > License. Here you will see the active status of your software and the date of next renewal.

2

Renewing Subscription

When your license is near expiry, click on "Renew License" to generate a payment request or contact your vendor directly.

[Screenshot: License Renewal Dialog]
3

Security Keys

Ensure your License Key is kept private. It is used to authenticate your school's specific cloud database instance.
