License & Subscription Guide

Secure your software investment and manage multi-school licenses.

Core Security

Product Activation

Upon installation, Nexus SMS requires a unique **Product Key** for activation. This key binds your software instance to your specific Google Cloud database. Never share this key with unauthorized personnel, as it grants full administrative access to your institution's data.

🛡️
Subscription

Expiry & Renewals

Your license is valid for a set term. You will see an 'Expiry Warning' on the main dashboard 30 days prior to the end of your term. To avoid service interruption, ensure you renew your subscription before the expiry date.

Multi-Tenant

Corporate Management

For large organizations managing multiple schools, the **License Control Plane** provides a centralized view of all branch performances, aggregate collection data, and global license distribution status.

NOTE: LICENSE UPGRADES

Upgrading your student limit does not require a software re-installation. Once your new tier is approved, the system limits will expand automatically in real-time across all your school branches.
