Screens and Workflows

Each card tells you exactly what the screenshot should show. Add images later with the same file names in the images folder.

Designing a Test This is a special academic menu where teachers can design new tests, choose which classes will be joining the challenge, and pick the perfect test day! It's a wonderful way to organize our learning goals, ensuring that every subject is explored and every student has a chance to show what they've discovered. By planning tests with such care, we create a clear path for growth and achievement in our magical school family.
The Test Calendar A comprehensive test calendar that lists every exam we've ever conducted, helping us look back at all the shimmering learning moments and milestones! This history is a treasure trove of information, allowing the school team to track academic progress over time and celebrate how much every student has grown. It's an essential tool for maintaining high standards and ensuring that our school remains a place of continuous discovery and excellence.
Score Keeper's Grid A clean, efficient, and super-fast entry grid where teachers can type in your shimmering test scores in the blink of an eye! This intuitive interface makes grading a breeze, allowing educators to focus more on providing helpful feedback and less on data entry. By simplifying the scoring process, we ensure that every student's hard work is recognized quickly and accurately, keeping everyone motivated on their path to success.
Your Golden Report Card A beautiful and official report card that our app creates just for you, filled with your marks, grades, and many happy, encouraging notes from your teachers! This document is a celebration of your learning journey, providing a detailed and professional summary of everything you've accomplished during the term. It's a proud moment for every student and family, reflecting the magic of hard work and the joy of reaching new heights in our school.
Seeing How We Shine An intelligent insights panel that shows exactly which subjects you are super at and where you can become even more of a superstar with a little more practice! This feature transforms numbers into helpful guidance, providing a clear map of your academic strengths and opportunities for growth. It's like having a friendly learning coach that always knows how to help you shine your brightest in every single lesson.
