Plug in the Megaphone This is where we connect the school's phone megaphone so it can start shouting happy news and important updates to all our parents! By setting up this connection, the school gains the power to reach out instantly, ensuring that every message is delivered directly to those who need to hear it. It's the starting point for great communication, helping to build a bridge between the school and every family in our magical community.
The History of Whispers Take a look through our 'History of Whispers,' a big digital book that shows every message we've ever sent and tracks if they reached our parent friends safely! This detailed log provides peace of mind, allowing the school team to verify that important alerts and celebratory news have been received as intended. By maintaining this record, we ensure that our communication is always reliable, transparent, and filled with the magic of shared information.
The Blinking News Center Check out the 'Blinking News Center,' a tiny and helpful box on the main dashboard that lights up whenever something important happens at school! Whether it's a new announcement or a reminder about an upcoming event, this little widget keeps you informed without you having to search for news. It's like having a friendly school herald that always makes sure you're the first to know about the latest magical updates and school surprises.
