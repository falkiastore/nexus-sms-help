Screens and Workflows

Each card tells you exactly what the screenshot should show. Add images later with the same file names in the images folder.

Your School's ID Card This is where you fill in your school's official name and phone number, and pick a shiny new logo to show off on every single page! It's like creating a special ID card for your school, ensuring that your unique brand is visible to everyone who uses the app. By keeping this profile updated, you make sure that every report and message carries your school's professional and magical identity.
Pick Your Paint Choose your school's favorite colors to make the app look exactly how you want it, transforming the digital workspace into a beautiful reflection of your school spirit! With our 'Pick Your Paint' feature, you can customize themes and styles to create a premium and welcoming environment for teachers and staff. It's a fun way to make the technology feel like a true part of your school's unique and colorful personality.
Building Classrooms Use this tool to add new grades and sections so every student friend has a cozy and organized place to learn and grow! 'Building Classrooms' allows you to map out your school's structure, ensuring that every group has a dedicated space in the digital registry. It's the foundation of a well-organized school, making it simple for everyone to find their way around the halls of our magical learning community.
Holiday Planner Mark all the fun holidays and important breaks on our big school calendar so everyone knows exactly when they get a well-deserved day off! This planner ensures that the school schedule is clear for the entire year, helping families and staff plan their own adventures with confidence. By keeping the 'Holiday Planner' updated, you keep the school's heartbeat steady and ensure that every celebration is anticipated with joy.
The Fee Rulebook Set the important rules for how much treasure goes into the school's piggy bank for each grade and section throughout the entire year! This rulebook ensures that fee policies are consistent, fair, and transparent for every single family in our school community. It's an essential part of our treasure management, helping the school stay healthy and ready to provide the best learning tools for every student.
